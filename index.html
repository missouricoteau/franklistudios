Who We Are

A Studio Built on Authentic Storytelling

Founded by filmmakers who believe the most powerful stories come from the margins, Frankli Studios is an independent production company dedicated to amplifying underrepresented voices and bold cinematic visions.

We partner with writers, directors, and creators at every stage — from developing a screenplay idea to delivering a finished film — combining artistic integrity with professional craft.

Our Services

Whether you need a full production partner or an experienced hand with your script, we bring passion and precision to every project.

Independent Film Production

End-to-end production for feature films, shorts, and documentaries. We handle pre-production planning, crew assembly, on-set direction, post-production, and distribution strategy — so your vision reaches the audience it deserves.

  • Feature & short film production
  • Documentary filmmaking

  • Post-production & color grading
  • Festival strategy & distribution

Screenwriting Services

From first draft to polished shooting script, our experienced writers and story consultants work alongside you to develop compelling characters, tight structure, and dialogue that crackles on the page.

  • Original screenplay development
  • Script coverage & analysis
  • Story consulting & structure
  • Dialogue polishing & rewrites
  • Pitching & treatment writing
